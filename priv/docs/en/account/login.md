Graasp offers multiple secure ways for users to access their accounts. This section explains how to log in using your email and password, along with tips for troubleshooting common issues.

## With Email {: #login-email}

You can log in to Graasp using your email address. This method will send you an email with a magic link.

**How it works:**

1. Go to the Graasp [login page](https://graasp.org/auth/login).
2. Enter the email address associated with your Graasp account.
3. Click **Receive a link to log in**.
4. You will receive a **magic link** in your inbox.
5. Open the email and click the link to be logged in automatically.

**Notes:**

- The magic link is valid for a limited time for security reasons.
- If you don’t receive the email, check your spam folder and verify that your email address is correct and has no typos.
- Some institutional email systems may block automated messages. Contact your administrator if needed.

## With Password {: #login-password}

If you previously set up a password to your account, you can log in using your email and password combination.

**Steps to log in:**

1. Go to the Graasp [login page](https://graasp.org/auth/login).
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**.
3. Click **Log in**.

If you forget your password, you can reset it at any time:

1. Click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter your email address.
3. Check your inbox for the reset instructions.
4. Create a new password following the recommended security guidelines.

**Troubleshooting tips:**

- Make sure caps lock is off when entering your password.
- If you don’t receive password reset emails, check your spam folder.
- For persistent issues, contact support at **support@graasp.org**.
