## Performance and Accessibility improvements for the landing page

We have made small changes to improve the performance and accessibility of the landing page. These include font fetching optimizations and link accessibility enhancements.

We also improved some technical aspects of our news feed. If you want to keep up to date with these update announcments you can subscribe to our RSS feed using your reader. Do not hesitate to reach out if you notice some issues with your reader.

These are related to our efforts to make our landing page faster and more accessible (also to search engines).

We are interested to get your opinion on the recent changes we made, if you want to share your thoughts with us, please contact us by email at: [feedback@graasp.org](mailto:feedback@graasp.org).

## Bug fixes

We fixed a bug that was affecting the home of some users. If you had access to an element with permission lower than admin in your home we would show you administrative features on this element enven though you could not permorm these. For example, deleting the element.
These actions were not autorized but it made it look like you could perform them.
We have fixed this and you should no longer see actions you are not authorized to perform.
